Agent战争的终局：从聊天界面到行动界面

ChatGPT代表的是"聊天界面"范式：你问，AI答，所有行动都需要你自己完成。微信小微代表的是"行动界面"范式：你说，AI做，它直接在你的生态里完成操作。这是一个根本性的交互范式转移。

这个转移对所有AI公司都有深远影响。如果你做的AI产品只是一个聊天框，你将面临超级App和操作系统的双重夹击——它们有用户、有场景、有数据、有系统权限，独立聊天App很难竞争。但如果你做的是垂直领域的Agent能力（比如帮小微更好地处理医疗咨询、帮大圆更好地做销售分析），你就有机会成为Agent生态里的"插件"。

Why "Xiaowei" Matters: A WeChat Agent Is a Different Species

There are already plenty of AI assistants on the market, but an Agent inside WeChat is an entirely different species.

The reason is that WeChat isn't an App -- it's an OS-level ecosystem. 1.6 billion users' social connections, payments, Mini Programs, Official Accounts, Video Accounts, and enterprise services are all inside. An Agent that can operate freely within the WeChat ecosystem means it can reply to messages, post Moments, order food, handle work approvals, manage group chats, and invoke Mini Programs for services -- all without jumping to other apps.

A user who tested Tencent's ad-placement Agent put it well: "Regular LLMs can only answer questions; Agents solve problems." The key to WeChat Xiaowei isn't "smarter chat" -- it's actually doing work in real business systems. When an Agent can call Mini Programs, complete payments, and operate WeCom approval workflows, it transforms from a toy into a genuine productivity tool.

WeCom "Dayuan's" logic is even more direct: in B2B scenarios, Agent value is easier to quantify. Auto-replying to customer inquiries, auto-generating meeting minutes, auto-approving routine workflows, auto-analyzing sales data -- these are all scenarios where ROI is directly measurable.

OS Agent-Native Transformation: Who Controls the Entry Controls the AI Era

WeChat building Agents is application-layer entry competition; Huawei HarmonyOS 7 and Apple Siri are system-layer entry competition.

Tell Xiaoyi "organize yesterday's meeting PPT and send it to Mr. Zhang," and it automatically finds the PPT app, opens the file, extracts key points, and sends via email or WeChat. Endgame of the Agent Wars: From Chat Interface to Action Interface

Looking at WeChat Xiaowei, WeCom Dayuan, HarmonyOS Xiaoyi, Apple Siri, and Doubao-Feishu collaboration together, a clear trend emerges: AI's interaction interface is shifting from "chat interface" to "action interface."

ChatGPT represents the "chat interface" paradigm: you ask, AI answers, all actions require you to complete them yourself. WeChat Xiaowei represents the "action interface" paradigm: you say, AI does, completing operations directly within your ecosystem. This is a fundamental interaction paradigm shift.

This shift has profound implications for all AI companies. If your AI product is just a chat box, you face a pincer attack from super-apps and operating systems -- they have users, scenarios, data, and system permissions; standalone chat apps will struggle to compete. But if you build vertical Agent capabilities (like helping Xiaowei better handle medical consultations, or helping Dayuan do better sales analytics), you have a chance to become a "plugin" in the Agent ecosystem.

Behind Tencent's single-day market cap swings of tens of billions of HKD is capital's revaluation of Agent entry-point value. The last time WeChat triggered this level of market cap reaction was when Video Accounts rose to prominence. Agents may be the biggest variable in the WeChat ecosystem since Video Accounts.

1.6 billion people's screens are becoming conversation interfaces. And whoever gets the first word in that conversation interface holds the ticket to the AI era.
